About

The Public Procurement Information Portal is an online platform provided by Public Procurement Regulatory Authority (PPRA) for publication of contract awards and tender notices by Procuring Entities. Procuring Entities should consolidate/upload the information on a monthly basis by the 15th day of every subsequent month.
